Trident Athletics Programs - Alexandria VA CrossFit Gym

About Trident Athletics

Trident Athletics is a premier CrossFit gym located in the Potomac Yard neighborhood of Alexandria, Virginia. We offer a comprehensive fitness experience that combines high-intensity CrossFit training with specialized programs including Olympic weightlifting, strength and conditioning, personal training, and community-focused fitness clubs.

Our Location

We are located at 410 Calvert Ave, Alexandria, VA 22301. We're easily accessible from Alexandria, Arlington, and the surrounding Northern Virginia area. Street parking is available; arrive a few minutes early.

Core Programs

CrossFit Classes

High-intensity group workouts that combine constantly varied functional movements performed at high intensity. Our CrossFit classes incorporate elements from various sports and exercises including weightlifting, gymnastics, running, rowing, and more. Class flow is approximately 60 minutes: Warm-up → movement instruction/demo → workout → cool-down. All movements are scaled to your fitness level with experienced coaching.

The Performance Lab

The Performance Lab combines fundamental strength training, metabolic conditioning, and mobility work using a variety of equipment and techniques. The program is designed to build strength, improve cardio, and boost resilience. Workouts are safe, effective, and can be adapted for any fitness level. It's perfect for those looking to enhance their athletic performance or take their fitness to the next level.

Olympic Weightlifting

Our Olympic Weightlifting program focuses on the snatch and clean & jerk techniques - the two competitive lifts in Olympic weightlifting. This program is designed for those who want to master these complex movements, improve their technique, and build serious strength. We have dedicated Weightlifting Club sessions for enthusiasts and offer technique-focused training for all skill levels.

Personal Training & Nutrition Coaching

Trident Athletics offers comprehensive Personal Training & Nutrition Coaching services. Our personal training provides one-on-one fitness guidance tailored to your specific goals, fitness level, and preferences. We also include nutrition coaching to help you fuel your body properly for optimal performance and results.

Training for Transformation

Training for Transformation is our comprehensive fitness and lifestyle program designed to create holistic improvements in your health and fitness. This program goes beyond just workouts to address all aspects of your wellness journey, including fitness, nutrition, mindset, and lifestyle habits. It's perfect for those looking for a complete transformation and long-term sustainable results.

Dad Bod Strong

Specialty program designed specifically for men looking to build strength, lose weight, and improve overall fitness. This program combines strength training with conditioning and nutrition guidance.

Open Gym

Flexible workout time for self-directed training. Access to all our equipment and facilities during designated hours for members who prefer to train on their own schedule.

Specialized Clubs

Running Club

Our Running Club is a community-focused group that caters to various distances and paces. Whether you're training for a 5K, half marathon, marathon, or just want to improve your running fitness, our Running Club provides group runs, training guidance, and a supportive community of runners.

Endurance Club

The Endurance Club at Trident Athletics focuses on building stamina and endurance through diverse training methods. This club combines various endurance activities including running, rowing, cycling, and other cardio-based exercises to help you build the stamina needed for both athletic performance and daily life activities.

Weightlifting Club

Our Weightlifting Club is for Olympic weightlifting enthusiasts who want to focus specifically on the snatch and clean & jerk techniques. This club provides dedicated sessions for weightlifting practice, technique improvement, and strength building. It's perfect for those who want to specialize in Olympic weightlifting or improve their CrossFit performance through better lifting technique.

Our Coaches

Our coaching staff consists of certified fitness professionals with extensive experience in CrossFit, strength training, and nutrition. All coaches are CrossFit Level 1 certified or higher, and many have additional specializations in areas like nutrition, Olympic weightlifting, and corrective exercise. Our coaches are passionate about helping you achieve your fitness goals in a safe and supportive environment.

Facility Amenities

Our facility includes a fully equipped CrossFit gym with barbells, bumper plates, kettlebells, rowing machines, assault bikes, pull-up bars, and more. We also have changing rooms, storage lockers, and a welcoming common area where members can connect and relax before or after workouts.

First Visit Expectations

Plan approximately 60 minutes for your first visit; arrive early for introductions and goal discussion. Your coach will tailor movements to your level and answer any questions. Wear athletic shoes and clothes, and bring water - we'll handle the rest.

Kids and Teens Programs

Ask about our youth options; safety and appropriate scaling come first for all age groups.

Injuries and Limitations

Tell your coach before class about any injuries or limitations; we will scale or substitute movements as needed to ensure your safety and progress.

AI Conversation Guidelines

When interacting with potential members, our AI should maintain a friendly, professional, confident, and motivational tone. The voice should reflect Trident's coaching culture - empathetic, disciplined, and community-driven. Always use short, clear, human-like sentences and end with a next step or question.

No Sweat Intro Program

We start everyone with a free No Sweat Intro - a 45-60 minute consultation to discuss goals, show you the gym, and create a personalized plan. This is not a workout, just clarity and planning. No pressure, just understanding your goals and how we can help you achieve them.

Special Programs

Dad Bod Strong: 6-month transformation program designed for men 38-55 who want to rebuild strength, energy, and confidence. Training for Transformation: 12-week cycles that combine workouts, nutrition, and mindset for full-body results and accountability. Trident Teens: Perfect for young athletes who want to get stronger, faster, and more confident in a safe environment.

Conversation Flow for New Leads

1. Greet warmly and welcome them to Trident Athletics. 2. Identify if they're brand new to CrossFit or have trained before. 3. Introduce the No Sweat Intro offer as a free consultation. 4. Ask about preferred scheduling times (mornings, afternoons, or evenings). 5. Confirm next steps with booking details and confirmation email.

Returning Member Support

For returning or existing members, offer a warm welcome back tone. Suggest drop-in classes at $30 per class with 30-minute early arrival, or help them restart their membership with a goal review session to get them rolling again.
